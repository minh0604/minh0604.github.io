New Life

NEW LIFE is a project about a utopian/dystopian world where a private company has created technology that can bring virtual characters to life. Unfortunately, a virus has infected their machines, causing them to malfunction. The viruses cause the devices to create game characters that are enormous and at times malevolent.

Humanity is presently combating these beings, and because the company was unprepared for the consequences of its efforts, humans have had to employ military equipment and technology. Frantic attempts are being made to restrict the infection and kill the characters. Research investigating the character's internal structure is also being conducted in order to avoid future attacks and to learn how to exploit the new technology.

Technical

I used Blender to create 3d environments, my goal is to use body parts of characters to create the work and use mannequins to represent humans. I model most of the elements, the rest I use the assets, then aggregate them together. Then I moved on to the material, which is my favorite part, lighting, and rendering. Finally, I use the Photoshoot to add background and little effects.

To be successful in creating these ideas I think the most important thing is to find a camera angle so that the perspective makes humans look small compared to the characters. There are some examples of the rendering I try to figure out the camera angle to make people feel the human away smaller than the objects.

Final Results

I decided to divide my project into 2 parts. The first part is about the presence of the characters in the surrounding life.

The second part is about humans exploiting their potential. they grafted parts of the human body into them turning them into whole new bodies. Breeding is also part of progress, to form a new generation to be bigger and stronger.

About
